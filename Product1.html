Everlasting Vanilla

Top Notes

Bergamot, Orange flower

Heart Notes

Aurantiol, Rose, Oakwood, Geranium

Base Notes

Vanilla, Amber, Sandalwood, Coconut

Description

A soft, luminous fragrance that unfolds like a warm memory on the skin.

It opens with a gentle sparkle of bergamot and delicate orange blossom nuances, touched by airy aldehydes that give a clean, almost glowing lift. This first impression feels light and elegant — like morning light filtering through sheer fabric.

At its heart, a tender floral harmony emerges. Velvety rose blends with soft, honeyed facets of aurantiol, creating a smooth, rounded bouquet. Subtle woody tones of oakwood and green floral accents add depth without disturbing the fragrance’s calm, graceful flow.

As it settles, the scent reveals its true soul — a rich, comforting base where creamy vanilla takes center stage. Layers of natural vanillin, warm amber, and smooth sandalwood melt together into a soft, enveloping warmth. A hint of benzyl benzoate enhances the sweetness and longevity, leaving a delicate, lasting trail that feels both intimate and timeless.
