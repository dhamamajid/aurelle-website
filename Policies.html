Returns

Returns are accepted within 14 days of delivery only if:

  • The product is unused

Opened fragrance products cannot be returned for hygiene and safety reasons. To initiate a return, please contact our customer service team with your order number and reason for return. We will provide instructions on how to return the item. Customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the return is due to a damaged or incorrect item.

If you receive a damaged or incorrect item, please contact our customer service team within 2 days of delivery with your order number and a photo of the issue. We will work to resolve the issue promptly, which may include sending a replacement or issuing a refund.

Refunds

Refunds for eligible returns will be processed within 7 business days of receiving the returned item. Refunds will be issued to the original payment method used for the purchase.
