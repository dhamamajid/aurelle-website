About us

At Aurelle, fragrance is not simply worn. It is experienced. It is a quiet expression of identity, a reflection of moments that shape us, and a subtle yet powerful connection between memory and emotion.

Founded in 2024 in Leuven, Belgium, Aurelle began as a personal vision, one rooted in passion, discipline, and the desire to create something meaningful. Built during student years and shaped by an immigrant background, the brand represents more than perfumery; it is the result of dedication, persistence, and the belief that something truly personal can resonate with others. Every free moment was invested into bringing Aurelle to life, with the intention of sharing something authentic with those who value depth, quality, and individuality.

Aurelle is defined by a minimal yet modern approach, balanced with a sense of understated luxury. We create fragrances for all genders, designed to accompany life’s most important moments. Whether bold, intimate, or unforgettable. Each scent is developed as a story, carefully composed to evoke emotion and create lasting impressions.

Our approach to perfumery is both creative and intentional. We focus on niche, experimental compositions, introducing new fragrances over timeeach with its own distinct character and identity. Rather than following trends, we explore originality, allowing each creation to stand on its own. Our perfumes are produced in small batches, ensuring attention to detail, exclusivity, and a consistently high standard of quality. When a fragrance resonates deeply, it may return, driven not by mass production, but by genuine demand.

Performance is at the core of every creation. Aurelle fragrances are crafted to be long-lasting, evolving throughout the day and leaving a refined, memorable trail. At the same time, each scent is designed to feel personal, adapting to the wearer and becoming uniquely theirs.

We believe that scent has the power to capture moments in a way nothing else can. A fragrance can remind you of a place, a person, or a feeling. Sometimes all at once. This belief is at the heart of Aurelle: creating perfumes that are not only worn, but remembered.

As we continue to grow, our vision is clear. To evolve into a trusted and recognized name within the world of luxury niche perfumery, while remaining true to our origins. Independence allows us to create freely, guided by passion rather than convention.

Aurelle is an invitation to explore, to feel, and to discover a fragrance that becomes part of your story.
